About Citizen Carz

Welcome to Citizen Carz, where passion meets precision in the world of premium pre-owned vehicles. Since our inception in 1998, we've been redefining the car buying experience, one satisfied customer at a time.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a space where car enthusiasts and everyday drivers alike could find their perfect match without compromising on quality or breaking the bank.

At Citizen Carz, we believe that everyone deserves to drive a car they love. That's why we meticulously handpick and thoroughly inspect each vehicle in our inventory, ensuring that only the cream of the crop makes it to our showroom floor.

Our Core Values

Quality Assurance

We rigorously inspect and certify every vehicle to ensure top-notch quality and reliability.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We go above and beyond to exceed your expectations.

Transparency

We pride ourselves on being transparent about vehicle histories, pricing, and any potential issues, building trust with our customers.

Diversity

We are dedicated to providing a diverse range of vehicle types and brands to cater to the unique needs and budgets of our customers.

Our History

Citizen Carz began as a small family business, driven by a shared love for automobiles and a commitment to exceptional customer service. Over the years, we've grown into one of the leading pre-owned car dealerships in the region, maintaining our core values while expanding our offerings to include a wider range of vehicles.

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who understand the automotive industry inside and out. We work tirelessly to create a seamless buying experience, from the moment you step into our showroom to the day you drive off in your new car.

Our Team

At Citizen Carz, our strength lies in our people. Our team of dedicated professionals brings together years of experience, knowledge, and expertise to assist you every step of the way. We take pride in fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation, ensuring that we are always up-to-date with the latest industry trends and customer preferences.

From our sales staff to our service technicians, each member of our team is committed to providing you with the best possible experience. We believe in continuous training and development to ensure that our team is always equipped to meet your needs.

Community Engagement

Being a responsible member of the community is a core aspect of our mission at Citizen Carz. We believe in giving back and actively participating in community events, charity drives, and local sponsorships.

Our community initiatives include organizing safe driving workshops, supporting local charities, and participating in environmental sustainability programs. We strive to make a positive impact and contribute to the well-being of our community.
